Explanation

The 2nd law of thermodynamics states that entropy in a closed system always tends to a maximum. This also means that the system spontaneously moves towards thermal equilibrium, where the temperature of the left box is the same as on the right. Your goal as the demon is to try and violate the 2nd law by selecting which particles can pass through the door, but be careful, you also generate entropy by using the door!
